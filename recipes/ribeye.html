Bourbon Rib-Eye Steak

Description

A style of cooking a rib-eye steak, using a bourbon whiskey marinade.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water.
  • 2/3 cup bourbon whiskey.
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce.
  • 1/4 cup of packed brown sugar.
  • 3 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce.
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice.
  • 4 (6 ounce) beef rib-eye steaks

Cooking Steps

(a) Preparing the marinade and marinading.

  1. Whisk together the water, bourbon whiskey, soy sauce, brown sugar, Worchestershire sauce, and lemon juice in a bowl, and pour into a plastic zipper bag.
  2. Add the rib-eye steaks, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag.
  3. Allow to marinate in the refrigerator for 8 hours or overnight.

(b) Grilling

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Remove the rib-eye steaks from the marinade, and shake off excess. Discard the remaining marinade. Grill the steaks on high, 1 to 2 minutes per side, to sear the meat. Move the steaks to a cooler part of the grill and cook for an additional 2 to 3 minutes per side, if desired.
